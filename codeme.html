CODE - ME

Program Benefits

A free social project that provides female youths with the technology, confidence, tools, knowledge and support to be creative about all they need to start and flourish in their choice of businesses as successful self-employed entrepreneurs or to get the job they want.

The program involves empowering female youths with the technology, skills and self-confidence they need to successfully start-up as SMEs entrepreneurs. The beneficiaries will be exposed to various software, packages and editors to learn how to create their own website and maintain it, design business cards, logo and letter heads, write proposals for their businesses and other self-developmental courses and activities . They won't have to pay extra cost to get the service elsewhere but save more and stay employed. The survey we carried has showed that most startup can't afford the services of a web developer, graphics designer and business consultants.

The project will underscore why it's vital and beneficial to have women in tech to tackle unemployment, gain access to credible workforce and secure long-term profitability for businesses. It will also build role models for the next generation of girls and young women.

HMI understand s that technology is a big part of building our world of today and the future. Thus, equal representation and engagement of girls and women across all areas of tech would ensure we have diversity of experiences and perspective to build a world that is more innovative and equal.

Especially as our live is becoming more and more technology-driven, it is important to ensure we have diversity within the active players advancing the technology. We are not just working towards having more women in tech, but also more women building tech.

Program Structure

-Web App Development: HTML & CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript and Jquery, PHP & MySQL, Wordpress, GitHub and Web Hosting.
-Leadership: Act of Leadership, Goal Setting and Emotional Intelligence.
-Entrepreneurship: Strategies for impact, Marketing, Building Impactful Business Plan and Model, Business Pitching, Social Entrepreneurship, Finance and Accounting.
