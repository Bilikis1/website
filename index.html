Why Women and Girls

Empowering women and girls is the right thing to do. Investing in them is one of smartest thing to do in other to promote a healthier, more prosperous and developed society. There are no limitations to what a woman can do when given the rightful opportunities. When empowered, educated and healthy, she can take care of herself and her family, she can build her own business even employ others and she can be a nation builder instead of being a burden to it. Every woman is powerful; she is an agent of change, she can change the world and make it a better place for all.

Who Whe Are

HMI is a Non-Profit Organization that focuses on promoting Equal opportunities, access to education, safe healthcare, Leadership advancement and self-reliance for women and girls in Nigeria. Our strategic signature projects and programs are tailored to provide the women and girls with interventions, empowerment, opportunities, capacity building and trainings to build self-esteem to develop and transform their communities. Investing in women and girls is the heart of our work because we know that investing in them will generate a powerful ripple effect leading to a healthier, more prosperous and developed society.

Our Vision

HMI envisions an Africa of hope, social justice and equality, where women and girls, irrespective of ethnicity, religious background and social status are delibrately uplifted and empowered to reach their full potentials and live their dreams.

Our Mission

We are here to nurture and empower women and girls in Nigeria to take charge of their lives and make positive contributions to their communities.
