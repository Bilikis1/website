    INVESTING IN WOMEN AND GIRLS IS THE HEART OF OUR WORK

    HMI is investing qualitative resources into ensuring that Nigerian women and girls are empowered towards the actualization of the gender angle the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

    Why Women and Girls

    Empowering women and girls is the right thing to do. Girls and women are always at the receiving end of gender discrimination, poverty, exploitation and vulnerable employment. Investing in them is one of smartest thing to do in other to promote a healthier, more prosperous and developed society. There are no limitations to what a woman can do when given the rightful opportunities. When empowered, educated and healthy, she can take care of herself and her family, she can builder own business even employ others and she can be a nation builder instead of being a burden to it. Every woman is powerful; she is an agent of change, she can change the world and make it a better place for all.

    Who We Are

    HMI(Her Matter Initiative) is a Non-Profit Organization that focuses on promoting Equal opportunities, access to education, safe healthcare, Leadership advancement and self-reliance for women and girls in Nigeria. Our strategic signature projects and programs are tailored to provide the women and girls with interventions, empowerment, opportunities, capacity building and trainings to build self-esteem to develop and transform their communities. Investing in women and girls is the heart of our work because we know that investing in them will generate a powerful ripple effect leading to a healthier, more prosperous and developed society.

    Our Vision

    HMI envisions an Africa of hope, social justice and equality, where women and girls, irrespective of ethnicity, religious background and social status are delibrately uplifted and empowered to reach their full potentials and live their dreams.

    Our Mission

    We are here to nurture and empower women and girls in Nigeria to take charge of their lives and make positive contributions to their communities.


    HMI acknowledges women and girls as 'Treasures' an indispensable facet of our dear continent. Therefore our passion to focus on women and girls is more than just a trend and itâ€™s non-optional-its Mission Critical.

    Our Uniqueness

    We are grassroots force of change, we deliver supports directly to the vulnerables in hard-to-reach communities and rural areas. We are deeply rooted in the grassroots, we are highly connected to the grassroots, we are the grassroots.

    Our Activities

    PROG
    (Promotion of Girls' Education)

    HMI project PROG is a long term continuous project that provides holistic support to poor girls in vulnerable communities. Targeted supports covers school fees, uniforms, school bags, writing materials (books, pencils, biro, etc), electronic learning devices like computers, teaching resources, such as stationery.

    Read more

    VOW
    (Vocational Skills For Rural Women)

    Project VOW is training rural women on various vocational skills and gives them technical support in form of advising on best suppliers, best markets, suggesting innovative activities to avoid market saturation and linking beneficiaries to each other so that they can complement each other's activities.

    Read more

    Female Youth Network (FYN)

    HMI deliberate passion to build and support a community of employable and self-reliant youths. FYN is growing to emerge as the foremost social hub that harnesses females' inherent potentials and capabilities, promoting gender equality, promotion of females' leadership development and inclusion, and building females' self-esteem.

    Read more

    Project HAP
    (Health Awareness Program)

    The goal of Project HAP (Health Awareness Program) is to promote healthy lifestyle amongst families residing in underserved and vulnerable communities by delivering sustainable interventions that lead to significant improvements in healthy practices, hygiene and restoration of dignity.
