    Female Youths Network

    HMI deliberate passion to build a community of employable and self-reliant youths

    Why Youths?

    When we think youth we think innovation, the combination of youth and innovation generate the perfect spark to drive positive change. With the appopriate exposure to technology, education, skills and good health, a young woman can build her business and even employ the others, she can make the world a better place.

    FYN has provided the platform to unite females from varied backgrounds and empower them to take charge of their lives and contribute to their community development.


    The Fyn group is driven by its core goal of being the foremost social hub harnessing females' inherent potentials and capabilities, promoting gender equality, promotion of females' leadership development and inclusion, and building females' self-esteem.


    Goal
    Our goal is to boost female youth capabilities towards advanced innovations and solutions to unemployment challenges.


    Our
    Uniqueness

    We are grassroots force of change, we deliver supports directly to the vulnerables in hard-to-reach communities and rural areas. We are deeply rooted in the grassroots, we are highly connected to the grassroots, we are the grassroots.

    Activities

    CODE - ME

    A social project that will provide female youths with the technology, confidence, tools, knowledge and support to be creative about all they need to start up their choice of businesses as successful self-employed entrepreneurs or to get the job they want.
