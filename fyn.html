When we think youth we think innovation, the combination of youth and innovation generate the perfect spark to drive positive change. Female Youths Network

FYN has provided the platform to unite females from varied backgrounds and empower them to take charge of their lives and contribute to their community development.


The Fyn group is driven by its core goal of being the foremost social hub harnessing females' inherent potentials and capabilities, promoting gender equality, promotion of females' leadership development and inclusion, and building females' self-esteem.

Goal

Our goal is to boost female youth capabilities towards advanced innovations and solutions to unemployment challenges.

Objectives

To offer educational tools and packages of linguistically and culturally based programmes in form of skills, workshops/seminars and conferences that will better their lives of Nigerian female youths, amplify their voices and transform them in them into community builders.


To provide a platform to unite women from varied backgrounds and all walks of life to exchange ideas, network and harmonize strategies for self-development.


To raise awareness, igniting dialogs and inspiring positive impact around human rights and fundamental freedoms of women and girls.


To participate in peace processes in troubled communities.
